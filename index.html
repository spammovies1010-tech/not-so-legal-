About Us

Guide-With-Elite is a mentorship program planned by Dr. Arsh (UCMS), where mentors are recruited very carefully to cover all kinds of student issues.

Born from the experience of cracking NEET 2025 - the most difficult medical entrance exam ever - our founders understand exactly what it takes to succeed when the stakes are highest.

We believe that "with proper guidance, you can get an even better rank" than what we achieved. That's why we've created this program to share the self-study methods, strategies developed after hundreds of attempts, and the elder-brother support every NEET aspirant deserves.

Why we started: We know the struggle of sticking to timetables and needing a different approach. Whether you're struggling with motivation, need perfect checklists, or want to use affordable online resources effectively - we've got you covered.

Future Vision: Although we're at a startup level, with your support we'll soon be expanding to engineering entrance (JEE) as well as board exams. We're also holding complimentary weekly Zoom meetings to address students with common issues.

Special Message for Repeaters: We have Aarish Ahmad, who took 3 attempts to reach MAMC - he knows exactly what to avoid in drop years and what to do. You're not alone in this journey!
